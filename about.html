About Ditswametsing FC

Ditswametsing FC is a club based in Moshupa, Botswana.

  • Founded: April 7, 2024
  • Stadium: Fish Arena
  • Capacity: 2000
  • President: Deker Motlogelwa
  • Manager: Kgosi Gorata
  • Founder: Sir Motlogelwa
  • Location: Moshupa, Maunatlala ward, Botswana
